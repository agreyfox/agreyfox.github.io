Approach方法

How the work grows.

工作如何展开。

Curiosity opens the problem. Engineering makes it reliable. AI multiplies effort only where judgment still belongs to people. Evidence and honest scope keep the system worth running.

以问题本身为起点；用工程保证可靠；仅在「判断仍应属于人」之处引入 AI。证据与诚实的边界，让系统值得长期运行。

  1. Start from the real constraint

    从真实约束出发

    Who is blocked, what breaks, which decision cannot be made. Tools come second.

    谁被卡住、哪里失效、哪个决策做不下去。工具永远排在问题之后。

    20+ years framing IT as problems to solve—not feature lists. 二十余年经验：把信息化当作待解问题，而非功能清单堆砌。

  2. Give it structure

    赋予结构

    Boundaries, flows, failure modes, feedback. One test: does it work in production?

    边界、流程、故障模式与反馈。唯一检验：上线之后是否仍能工作？

    Full-stack delivery: design → build → operate—not slideware handoffs. 全栈交付：设计 → 实现 → 运维，而非甩文档式交接。

  3. Use AI as leverage

    以 AI 作杠杆

    Models search, draft, and challenge. People still own the decision. Review should feel natural—not a checkbox after damage is done.

    模型负责检索、起草与质疑；决策仍在人。审核应是流程中的自然步骤，而非事后补救的勾选。

    fmr: metrics enter the archive only after human confirm. Product → fmr：指标须经人工确认后方可入库。产品站 →

  4. Keep evidence close

    紧贴证据

    Logs beat narratives. Missing evidence is a finding, not a footnote to skip.

    日志胜过叙事。证据缺失本身就是发现，不是可以跳过的脚注。

    Requirements and acceptance criteria written so they can be tested. 需求与验收标准写到可被测试、可被复核。

  5. Respect incentives

    尊重真实激励

    Systems fail when they ignore how people actually behave. Map stakeholders before architecture freezes.

    忽视真实行为的系统终将失败。架构定型之前，先理清利益相关方与激励结构。

    Workshops that surface incentives early—before the wrong system is built. 在错误系统成形前，通过研讨尽早暴露激励与阻力。

  6. Leave judgment human

    判断留给人

    What should technology serve? Where must a person stay in the loop? Those questions sit next to the backlog—not on a separate ethics slide.

    技术服务谁？何处必须有人在环？这些问题与待办并列，而不是单独一页「伦理声明」。

    No silent auto-commit of sensitive AI output. 敏感 AI 结果禁止在无人确认时静默写入。
