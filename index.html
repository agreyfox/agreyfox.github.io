How I work

我如何工作

Start from the constraint. Structure with engineering. Use AI only where judgment stays human. Keep evidence close.

从约束出发，用工程搭结构，只在判断仍应属于人的地方用 AI，并始终贴近证据。

  1. Start from the real constraint

    从真实约束出发

    Who is blocked, what breaks, which decision cannot be made. Tools come second.

    谁被卡住、哪里失效、哪个决策做不下去。工具永远排在问题之后。

    20+ years framing IT as problems, not feature lists 二十余年：把信息化当作问题，而非功能清单

  2. Give it structure

    赋予结构

    Boundaries, flows, failure modes, feedback. The test is production, not the slide deck.

    边界、流程、故障模式与反馈。检验标准是生产环境，不是幻灯片。

    Full-stack: design → build → operate 全栈：设计 → 实现 → 运维

  3. Use AI as leverage

    以 AI 作杠杆

    Models search, draft, and challenge. People own the decision. Review should feel natural.

    模型检索、起草、质疑；决策在人。审核应是流程中的自然步骤。

    fmr saves metrics only after confirm · product fmr 指标须确认后入库 · 产品站

  4. Keep evidence close

    紧贴证据

    Logs beat narratives. Missing evidence is a finding, not a footnote to skip.

    日志胜过叙事。证据缺失本身就是发现，不是可以跳过的脚注。

    Acceptance criteria written to be tested 验收标准写到可测试、可复核

  5. Respect incentives

    尊重真实激励

    Systems fail when they ignore how people behave. Map stakeholders before architecture freezes.

    忽视真实行为的系统终将失败。架构定型前先理清利益相关方。

    Incentive mapping before build 建设前先做激励映射

  6. Leave judgment human

    判断留给人

    Where must a person stay in the loop? Keep that next to the backlog, not on a separate ethics slide.

    何处必须有人在环？与待办并列，而不是单独一页伦理声明。

    No silent auto-commit of sensitive AI output 敏感 AI 结果禁止静默自动写入
