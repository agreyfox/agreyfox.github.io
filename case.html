fmr - Family Medical Records

Local-first encrypted archive for family lab reports. AI assists extraction; people confirm before save.

面向家庭化验资料的本地优先加密档案。AI 辅助提取；入库前必须人工确认。

Problem问题

  • Lab PDFs and photos scatter across phones
  • Cloud health apps raise privacy concerns
  • OCR dumps numbers without a clear review step
  • 化验 PDF 与照片散落在手机中
  • 云端健康应用带来隐私顾虑
  • OCR 直接抛出数值，缺少审核步骤

Approach做法

  • Encrypted local storage by default
  • Import, AI parse, editable table, confirm
  • BYOK, local Ollama, or limited free demo
  • 默认本地加密存储
  • 导入、AI 解析、可编辑表格、确认入库
  • 自备密钥、本地 Ollama，或有限免费演示

Proof points可核验要点

Human-in-the-loop: no silent auto-commit of AI metrics.

人在环路：AI 指标禁止无人确认时静默入库。

Privacy-first: raw files stay on device unless the user opts into cloud.

隐私优先：原始文件默认留在本机；仅用户主动选择时走云端。

Stack: Flutter, Riverpod, Drift/SQLCipher, go_router.
