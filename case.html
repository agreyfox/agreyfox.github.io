fmr — Family Medical Records

Local-first encrypted archive for family lab reports. AI assists extraction; humans confirm before anything is saved.

One-pager · Case snapshot
Developer: agreyfox
Package: com.agreyfox.fmr

Problem

  • Family lab PDFs/photos scatter across phones and folders
  • Cloud health apps raise privacy concerns
  • OCR/AI dumps metrics without a clear review step

Approach

  • Local encrypted storage by default
  • Import → AI parse → editable table → confirm
  • AI paths: BYOK → local Ollama → limited free demo

Role

  • Solo · product · design · engineering
  • IT consultant / full-stack / AI collaborator
  • 20+ years information systems background

Status

  • Android app in active development
  • Play listing prep · membership UI coming soon
  • Product site + Privacy live on GitHub Pages

Proof points

Human-in-the-loop: no silent auto-commit of AI metrics.

Privacy-first: raw medical files stay on device unless the user opts into a cloud path.

Stack: Flutter · Riverpod · Drift/SQLCipher · go_router.
